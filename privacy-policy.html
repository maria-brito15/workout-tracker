Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

At Workout Tracker, I take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle your information when you use my application.

Data Storage

Workout Tracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage technology. This means:

  • Your workout data never leaves your device.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • I do not collect, store, or have access to your personal information.
  • There are no user accounts, tracking, or analytics.

What Data is Stored

The application stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Exercise library entries (names and notes).
  • Workout presets you create.
  • Workout session logs (dates, exercises, sets, weights, reps).
  • Theme preferences.

Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can export all your data at any time using the Export function.
  • You can delete your data by clearing your browser's localStorage or using the delete all data feature.
  • You can transfer your data between devices using the Import/Export features.

Third-Party Services

Workout Tracker does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The application operates entirely client-side.

Cookies

This application does not use cookies. All preferences and data are stored using localStorage.

Changes to Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit my Contact page.
