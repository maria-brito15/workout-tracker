About Workout Tracker

Workout Tracker is a minimalist, privacy-focused fitness application designed for individuals who want to take control of their strength training journey without compromising their personal data.

Our Mission

We believe that tracking your fitness progress should be simple, effective, and private. That's why we created an application that runs entirely in your browser, storing all your data locally on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking—just you and your workout data.

How to Use

Getting started with Workout Tracker is simple. Follow these steps to begin tracking your fitness journey:

1. Build Your Exercise Library

Start by going to the Library tab and clicking "New Exercise". Add your favorite exercises with optional notes for form cues, equipment settings, or personal bests. This creates your personal exercise database that you'll use throughout the app.

2. Create Workout Presets

Navigate to the Presets tab and click "New Preset". Select exercises from your library and give your routine a name (e.g., "Push Day", "Leg Day", "Upper Body"). Presets make it quick and easy to start tracking your regular workout routines.

3. Track Your Workouts

Go to the Workouts tab and click "Track Workout". Select a preset or create a custom workout, then log each set with weight and reps. You can mark sets as warmup or working sets, and optionally add cardio details with type and duration.

4. Use the Rest Timer

Switch to the Timer tab during your workout. Use quick-set intervals (30s, 1m, 1m 30s, 2m, 3m) or enter a custom duration. Start, pause, and reset as needed to optimize your rest periods between sets.

5. Manage Your Data

Use the Export button to download your data as a JSON backup file. Use Import to restore data from a previous export or transfer data between devices. Toggle between dark and light themes using the Theme button.

Install as an App (Mobile)

Want quick access to Workout Tracker right from your home screen? You can install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your phone:

For Android (Chrome/Edge)

1. Open this website in Chrome or Edge.
2. Tap the three dots menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.
3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".
4. Confirm the installation.
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen like a native app!

For iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1. Open this website in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Name the app and tap "Add".
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

Benefits of installing: Works offline after initial load, faster access, full-screen experience without browser UI, and feels like a native app!

Why this Workout Tracker?

In an era where most fitness apps require accounts, collect user data, and operate behind subscription paywalls, Workout Tracker stands apart. We prioritize:

Privacy: Your workout data belongs to you and stays on your device.

Simplicity: A clean, distraction-free interface that focuses on what matters—your training.

Accessibility: Free to use, works offline (requires internet to load, but saves all data locally on your device.), and requires no installation or signup.

Control: Complete ownership of your data with easy export and import functionality.
