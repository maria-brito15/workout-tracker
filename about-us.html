About Workout Tracker

Workout Tracker is a minimalist, privacy-focused fitness application designed for individuals who want to take control of their strength training journey without compromising their personal data.

Our Mission

We believe that tracking your fitness progress should be simple, effective, and private. That's why we created an application that runs entirely in your browser, storing all your data locally on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking—just you and your workout data.

How to Use

Getting started with Workout Tracker is simple. Follow these steps to begin tracking your fitness journey:

1. Build Your Exercise Library

Start by going to the Library tab and clicking "New Exercise". Add your favorite exercises with optional notes for form cues, equipment settings, or personal bests. You can also add tags to your exercises to easily filter and organize them by muscle group or equipment.

2. Create Workout Presets

Navigate to the Presets tab and click "New Preset". Select exercises from your library and give your routine a name (e.g., "Push Day", "Leg Day", "Upper Body"). Presets make it quick and easy to start tracking your regular workout routines with a single click.

3. Track Your Workouts

Go to the Workouts tab and click "Track Workout". Select a preset or create a custom workout. You can log each set with weight and reps, and classify them as warm-up (W), normal (N), or failure (F). You can also track cardio sessions with duration and type.

4. Use the Rest Timer

Switch to the Timer tab during your workout. Use quick-set intervals (30s, 1m, 1m 30s, 2m, 3m) or enter a custom duration. The timer helps you maintain consistent rest periods, which is crucial for progressive overload and recovery.

5. Manage Your Data

Use the Export button to download your entire workout history and library as a JSON backup file. Use Import to restore data from a previous export or sync your progress between different devices.

Install as an App (Mobile)

Workout Tracker is a Progressive Web App (PWA), meaning you can install it directly on your phone for a native app experience:

For Android (Chrome/Edge)

1. Open this website in Chrome or Edge.
2. Tap the three dots menu (⋮).
3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".
4. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

For iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1. Open this website in Safari.
2. Tap the Share button.
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap "Add" to confirm.

Key Features

Exercise Library & Tags

Organize your exercises with custom tags and detailed notes for every movement.

Flexible Routines

Use presets for consistency or build custom workouts on the fly when you want to change things up.

Advanced Logging

Track sets, reps, weight, and set types (Warmup, Normal, Failure) alongside cardio sessions.

Integrated Rest Timer

Stay focused with a built-in timer featuring presets and custom duration settings.

Privacy First

No accounts, no tracking. All data is stored 100% locally in your browser's storage.

Offline Access

Thanks to Service Workers, the app works even when you don't have an internet connection.

Why Choose Workout Tracker?

Most fitness apps today are bloated with social features, require expensive subscriptions, and harvest your personal data. Workout Tracker is different:

• 100% Free: No hidden fees or "premium" features.

• No Data Collection: We don't even have a server to send your data to. It's yours, and it stays with you.

• Lightweight: Built with vanilla JavaScript for maximum speed and minimal battery drain.

• Ownership: Easily export your data to JSON anytime for your own records or analysis.
