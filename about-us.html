About Workout Tracker

How to Use

Getting started with Workout Tracker is simple. Follow these steps to begin tracking your fitness journey:

1. Build Your Exercise Library

Start by going to the Library tab and clicking "New Exercise". Add your favorite exercises with structured detail fields for last weight used, personal record (PR), warm-up weight, and set specification, plus a free-text field for form and execution notes. You can also add tags to organize exercises by muscle group or equipment for quick filtering.

2. Create Workout Presets

Navigate to the Presets tab and click "New Preset". Use the built-in search to quickly find and select exercises from your library, then give your routine a name (e.g., "Push Day", "Leg Day", "Upper Body"). Presets make it quick and easy to start tracking your regular workout routines with a single click.

3. Track Your Workouts

Go to the Workouts tab and click "Track Workout". Select a preset or create a custom workout. Log each set with weight and reps, and classify them as warm-up, normal, or failure. Tap any exercise name to instantly open its detail panel ‚Äî showing your last weight, PR, warm-up weight, set spec, and form notes ‚Äî without leaving the workout. When you finish a set, tap the "‚úì Rest" button to instantly start your rest timer. You can also track cardio sessions with duration and type.

4. Use the Rest Timer

A rest timer is available in two ways. During a workout, tap "‚è± Rest Timer" at the top of the workout view ‚Äî a floating panel slides in from the bottom-right with a circular countdown, preset durations (1m, 90s, 2m, 3m, 5m), and a custom input. Your preferred duration is remembered between sessions. You can also access the full standalone timer via the Timer tab at any time.

5. Manage Your Data

Use the Export button to download your entire workout history and library as a JSON backup file. Use Import to restore data from a previous export or sync your progress between devices. Need a clean slate? The Delete All button (in red) permanently wipes all data after a confirmation prompt.
