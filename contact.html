Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?
Yes! All your data is stored locally in your browser. We never collect or transmit your personal information.

Can I use this on multiple devices?
Yes, you can export your data from one device and import it on another using the Import/Export features.

Is Workout Tracker free?
Yes, Workout Tracker is completely free and open-source with no hidden fees or subscriptions.

Does it work offline?
Requires internet to load, but saves all data locally on your device.
